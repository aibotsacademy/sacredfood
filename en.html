🌱Abya Yala🛰️ Food Security for Indigenous Communities

1️⃣ It is urgent to achieve the full inclusion of indigenous peoples in the fulfillment of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the United Nations 2030 Agenda.

2️⃣ Indigenous food systems are particularly nutritious, remarkably resilient to climate, and well adapted to the environment.

3️⃣ This is why we created an indigenous agricultural platform using AI for satellite image analysis combined with hardware on the ground to achieve the goal of Zero Hunger.
