DETECTED DATA

The monitoring system can detect a large amount of data and is predisposed to continually expand.

Temperature

Temperature is a quantity referred to the notion of heat measurable by a thermometer

Air humidity

It is the vapor pressure exerted by the water vapor contained in a volume of saturated air at the temperature of the air contained in that volume.

Humidity of floor

Soil moisture is the amount of water per volume of land that is in a field.

pH

The pH is a scale designed to measure the alkalinity or acidity of a substance, and indicates the percentage of hydrogen contained in it.

Air quality

The air quality index is a figure provided by the authorities of an area that reflects the amounts of pollutants present in the air.

Connectivity

Connectivity is the ability of a device to connect and communicate with another, in order to exchange information or establish a direct connection based on digital information.

Hardware

Hardware was built which will have a constant monitoring purpose, which will provide us with real-time data on subsoil pH levels, humidity data, ambient temperature, air quality, subsoil humidity data.
